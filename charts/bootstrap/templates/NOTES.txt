The bootstrap chart has created ArgoCD applications to manage your infrastructure and applications.

  kubectl get applications -n argocd

  kubectl describe application {{ .Values.applications.infrastructure.name }} -n argocd
  kubectl describe application {{ .Values.applications.applications.name }} -n argocd

You can also check the status through the ArgoCD UI:
  
  https://localhost:8080
